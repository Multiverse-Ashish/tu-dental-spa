Patient Resources

Find answers to common questions about treatment plans, booking schedules, and concierge policies.

Your first visit includes a detailed dental scanning diagnostic, digital photo alignment mapping, and a comprehensive wellness conversation with Dr. Evelyn Stone to map out your long-term smile architecture. Expect 60–90 minutes of unhurried, private consultation.
Absolutely. We provide custom interest-free payment plans starting at 0% APR for 12 months. We also work directly with premium PPO provider networks and CareCredit to optimize patient coverage and reduce out-of-pocket costs.
We compile high-resolution 3D digital simulation models before beginning orthodontic aligners or veneers. This lets you preview alignment changes, tooth contours, and shade gradients from every angle — with full revision opportunities before any clinical work starts.
With proper care and regular maintenance visits, high-quality feldspathic porcelain veneers at Tu Dental SPA typically last 12–20 years. We use only lab-certified, custom-milled porcelain from our partner ceramics studio for each patient's unique tooth profile.
The virtual consultation is a completely non-obligatory, complimentary 20-minute video session. You review your digital smile simulation with Dr. Stone and ask questions. There is no commitment to treatment until you formally sign your personalized care plan.
After guided implant surgery, most patients return to regular daily activity within 2–3 days. Osseointegration (the bone fusing process) takes 3–6 months, during which a temporary restoration is fitted. The final zirconia crown is placed once integration is confirmed via 3D cone beam imaging.
Yes. Tu Dental SPA is designed specifically to alleviate clinical anxiety. We offer noise-cancelling headphones, aromatherapy protocols, and optional oral sedation administered by our licensed sedation specialist. Our private suites eliminate exposure to other patients, creating a truly spa-like experience throughout your visit.
We operate as a boutique concierge studio and kindly require appointments to maintain exceptional service standards. However, dental emergencies are accommodated on the same day when slots are available. Call our studio directly or use WhatsApp for priority emergency booking.
